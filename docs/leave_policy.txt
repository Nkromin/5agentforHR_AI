LEAVE POLICY

Section 1: Annual Leave Entitlement
- All full-time employees are entitled to 20 days of paid annual leave per year
- Leave days are accrued at 1.67 days per month
- Unused leave can be carried forward up to 5 days to the next year
- Leave requests must be submitted at least 2 weeks in advance
- Manager approval is required for all leave requests

Section 2: Sick Leave Entitlement
- All employees are entitled to 12 sick leave days per year
- Sick leave is fully paid
- A medical certificate is required for absences of 3 or more consecutive days
- Sick leave cannot be carried forward to the next year
- For fever, cold, or other illnesses, employees may use sick leave
- Notify your manager as soon as possible when taking sick leave

Section 3: Maternity and Paternity Leave
- Maternity Leave: 26 weeks of paid leave for birth mothers
- Paternity Leave: 4 weeks of paid leave for fathers
- Adoption Leave: 12 weeks of paid leave for adoptive parents
- Leave can be taken in continuous or split periods with manager approval
- Notify HR at least 8 weeks before expected leave start date

Section 4: Other Leave Types
- Bereavement Leave: 5 days for immediate family members
- Jury Duty: Paid leave for the duration of jury service
- Personal Leave: 3 days per year for personal matters
- Marriage Leave: 5 days for employee's own marriage
- Study Leave: Up to 10 days per year for approved courses
