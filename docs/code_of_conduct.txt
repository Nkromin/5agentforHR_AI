CODE OF CONDUCT

Section 1: Professional Behavior
- Treat all colleagues with respect and dignity
- Maintain a harassment-free workplace at all times
- Report any misconduct to HR or use anonymous ethics hotline
- Dress appropriately for your role and meetings
- Be punctual for meetings and deadlines

Section 2: Conflicts of Interest
- Disclose any potential conflicts of interest to your manager immediately
- Do not accept gifts over â‚¹5000 from vendors or clients
- Do not engage in outside employment that conflicts with company interests
- Recuse yourself from decisions where you have personal interest
- Report any family relationships with vendors or clients

Section 3: Confidentiality
- Protect company confidential information at all times
- Sign NDA before accessing sensitive projects
- Do not discuss confidential matters in public places
- Return all confidential materials upon leaving the company
- Do not share client information with third parties

Section 4: Social Media Guidelines
- Do not share confidential company information on social media
- Clearly state opinions are personal and not the company's views
- Do not disparage the company or colleagues online
- Report any social media attacks on the company to PR team
- Get approval before representing company on social media

Section 5: Disciplinary Action
- First offense: Verbal warning and documentation
- Second offense: Written warning with improvement plan
- Third offense: Final written warning and probation
- Fourth offense or severe misconduct: Termination of employment
- Severe misconduct includes theft, harassment, fraud, or violence
