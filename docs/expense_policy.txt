EXPENSE REIMBURSEMENT POLICY

Section 1: Eligible Expenses
- Business travel including flights, hotels, and ground transportation
- Client entertainment including meals and events
- Office supplies when working remotely
- Professional development including conferences and courses
- Mobile phone bills if used for work purposes

Section 2: Domestic Travel Expense Limits
- Domestic flights: Economy class for all flights
- Hotel accommodation: Up to ₹8000 per night for domestic travel
- Meals: Up to ₹1500 per day for domestic travel
- Ground transportation: Actual costs with receipts
- Client entertainment: Up to ₹3000 per person with prior approval

Section 3: International Travel Expense Limits
- International flights: Business class for flights over 8 hours
- Hotel accommodation: Up to ₹15000 per night for international travel
- Meals: Up to ₹2500 per day for international travel
- Ground transportation: Actual costs with receipts
- Client entertainment: Up to ₹5000 per person with prior approval

Section 4: Submission Process
- Submit expenses within 30 days of incurring them
- Include original receipts for all expenses over ₹500
- Use the expense management system for all submissions
- Manager approval required for all expense reports
- Reimbursement processed within 2 weeks of approval

Section 5: Non-Reimbursable Expenses
- Personal entertainment and leisure activities
- Alcohol unless part of approved client entertainment
- First-class flights without prior approval
- Personal travel extensions
- Expenses without valid receipts
- Mini-bar charges in hotels
