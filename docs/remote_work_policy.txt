REMOTE WORK POLICY

Section 1: Eligibility Requirements
- Employees must complete 6 months probation period before requesting remote work
- Role must be suitable for remote work as determined by department head
- Manager approval required for all remote work arrangements
- Employees must have demonstrated reliable performance and communication
- Remote work privilege can be revoked for performance issues

Section 2: Remote Work Guidelines
- Maximum 3 days per week remote work allowed for eligible employees
- Core hours: 10 AM to 3 PM - must be available online during these hours
- Must attend all required meetings whether virtual or in-person
- Company laptop and VPN access required for all remote work
- Home office must meet safety and ergonomic standards
- Employees must have stable internet connection minimum 25 Mbps

Section 3: Equipment for Remote Work
- Company provides laptop, external monitor, keyboard, and mouse
- Employee responsible for internet connection costs
- IT support available during business hours 9 AM to 6 PM
- Report any equipment issues to IT within 24 hours
- All company equipment must be returned if remote work ends

Section 4: Security Requirements for Remote Work
- Use company VPN for all work activities
- Do not use public WiFi for accessing company systems
- Keep work devices in secure location
- Enable screen lock when stepping away
- Do not allow family members to use work devices
